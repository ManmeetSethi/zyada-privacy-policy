Cancellation and Refund Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026

1. Scope

This policy applies to paid Zyada subscriptions and digital services purchased through the app or a supported checkout provider. Zyada is operated by Manmeet Sethi as an individual developer.

2. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through the payment provider, app store, customer portal, or by contacting support at m20sethi@gmail.com. Cancellation stops future renewal charges but does not automatically refund amounts already paid.

3. Access After Cancellation

Unless otherwise required by the payment provider or applicable law, you may continue to access paid features until the end of the paid billing period. After that, access may be downgraded or disabled.

4. Refunds

Zyada is a digital subscription service. Refunds are generally not issued once paid access has started. We may review refund requests for duplicate charges, incorrect charges, failed entitlement activation, or technical issues that prevent reasonable use of the service.

To request a refund, email m20sethi@gmail.com within 7 days of the charge and include your registered phone number, payment ID or invoice ID, charge date, and a short description of the issue.

5. Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed through the original payment provider. Bank, UPI, card, or provider settlement timelines may vary and are outside Zyada's direct control.

6. Provider and App Store Rules

If you purchased through Google Play, Razorpay, Dodo Payments, or another payment provider, additional cancellation, refund, tax, chargeback, or dispute rules from that provider may apply.

7. Contact

Cancellation or refund questions: m20sethi@gmail.com.
