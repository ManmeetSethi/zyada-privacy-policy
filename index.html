Zyada Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 June 2026

Zyada is a mobile app that helps Indian local businesses grow their Google reviews by finding their Google listing, comparing review counts, sending review-request links, and tracking review growth.

This policy explains what data Zyada collects, why we collect it, who we share it with, how we protect it, and how you can contact us or withdraw consent. Zyada is operated by Manmeet Sethi as an individual developer. For privacy questions or data requests, email m20sethi@gmail.com.

1. Data We Collect

Data category Examples Why we use it
Account and contact data Your mobile number (you enter or confirm it; it may be pre-filled from your shop's public Google listing) and an anonymous account identifier To create your account, associate your app data with you, and contact you. The app signs you in anonymously — we do not verify your number by OTP.
Business listing data Business name, address, Google Place ID, rating, review count, business category, city, and approximate listing coordinates To identify your business, compare review counts, generate Google review links, and track review growth.
Customer contact data you enter Customer name, phone number, and whether you sent a review link To help you send review-request messages and track your weekly review-request activity.
Payment and subscription data Subscription ID, payment status, plan, billing period, and webhook events To activate, renew, cancel, and verify your paid subscription. We do not store card, UPI, or bank details.
App activity and analytics Onboarding steps, sign-in completion, purchase events, app launches, and feature usage events (via Mixpanel and Meta) To understand whether the app is working, improve the product, and measure advertising performance.
Advertising identifier Android Advertising ID (GAID) and Meta app-event identifiers To measure whether Meta ads led to app installs, registration, and purchases, and to attribute conversions. You can limit or reset this in your device's Google → Ads settings.
Diagnostics and device data Crash logs, error traces, device model, operating system, app version, and basic performance data To find bugs, diagnose errors, and keep the app reliable.
Push notification token Expo push token and notification delivery metadata To notify you about review growth or new-review alerts if notifications are enabled.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide the app's core features: business search, review links, competitor comparison, review tracking, customer reminders, and reply drafts.
  • Authenticate users and protect account-scoped data.
  • Process and verify subscriptions.
  • Send or prepare review-request messages when you choose to do so.
  • Monitor app reliability and fix technical issues.
  • Measure advertising and campaign performance, including via a device advertising identifier (GAID).

We do not sell your personal data.

3. Customer Contacts You Add

When you add a customer's name or phone number, you confirm that the person is your customer and that you may contact them about your business. Zyada processes those customer contacts on your behalf so you can send review-request links. Do not add a person's phone number unless you have a valid relationship and permission to contact them.

5. Advertising and Measurement

Zyada uses Meta app events and a device advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID / GAID) to measure app installs, registrations, and purchases and to attribute them to our advertising. You can limit ad tracking, reset your advertising ID, or opt out of ads personalization at any time from your device's Settings → Google → Ads. We also use Mixpanel for first-party product analytics (how the app is used to improve it); Mixpanel does not use the advertising identifier.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep account, business, customer, send-event, and subscription data while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. We may retain limited records for legal, fraud-prevention, tax, accounting, or dispute-resolution purposes. You can request access, correction, withdrawal of consent, or deletion by emailing m20sethi@gmail.com.

7. Security

We use encryption in transit, account-scoped access controls, and database row-level security to protect user data. Payment credentials such as card, UPI, and bank details are handled by Razorpay and are not stored on Zyada servers.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can decline ad measurement. The app will still work.
  • You can turn ad measurement off later in Settings.
  • You can request access, correction, deletion, or withdrawal of consent by email.
  • You can reset local app data from Settings. Server-side deletion requests should be sent by email.

9. Children's Privacy

Zyada is intended for business owners and is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when the app, providers, or legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
