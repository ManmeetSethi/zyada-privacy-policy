# Zyada Legal Pages

Public GitHub Pages host for Zyada mobile app legal and support pages.

Important: the root URL (`https://manmeetsethi.github.io/zyada-privacy-policy/`) is the Play Console
privacy-policy URL and must render the privacy policy directly. Do not replace the root with a legal-pages
index or marketing page. Additional legal/support pages are linked from `legal-pages.html` and their own
stable paths.

- Legal page index: `legal-pages.html`
- Terms and Conditions: `terms-and-conditions.html`
- Privacy Policy copy: `privacy-policy.html`
- Cancellation and Refund: `cancellation-and-refund.html`
- Shipping and Exchange: `shipping-and-exchange.html`
- Contact Us: `contact-us.html`
